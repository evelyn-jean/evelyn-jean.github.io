Patterns

Most crocheters find what they are looking to make through patters either online or in text. Experienced crocheters may have the ability to create their own patterns, able to make what ever they want if they can figure out how. It can be pretty difficult with a lot of trial and error, so if that’s not your thing, then don’t worry. There are millions of patterns out there.

Most patterns need to be paid for, but there are many patterns that can be found online for free.

Be sure to know which terminology you are working with before starting (US/UK). I know this from experience.
